A Simple Setup to Connect Volunteers and Volunteer Opportunities.

Community Outreach

Our vision is simple. We want people who are looking for volunteer opportunities to be able to get matched with organizations and events within their community that fit their schedule and interests. The key features of our application is its ability to coordinate organizations, their events, and interested community volunteers.

Future work will include a smart matching service that allows volunteers within the community to be automatically matched with events that are both relevant to them and at times when they are available.



Easy to Install

StepUP is a platform based community tool. Different organizations, such as school PTAs, church groups, and even school clubs, can setup their own community where they can add sub-organizations and volunteering opportunities. The organization then has its own dedicated user base of community members who can be matched to events.

Easy to Use

StepUP is a one stop shop tool for volunteering within a community. Users can create profiles where they can ask to be added to different communities or can be added by a community based off on their skills, credentials, or level of trust within that community.

The recommendation and easy to use smart search will also try to match different volunteers to volunteer events and vice versa.

Easy to Manage

For a user, once a profile is made it is easy to manage. Profiles will eventually be able to be imported from Linkedin and be updated that way. For a community/orginization, adding and removing people within their trusted sub-community can be done with a click of a button.
