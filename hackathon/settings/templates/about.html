A Simple Setup to Connect Volunteers and Volunteer Opportunities.

Community Outreach

Our vision is simple. We want people who are looking for volunteer opportunities to be able to set up a profile and have the ability to view organizations and events within their community that are interested in and schedule. The key feature of our application is smart matching feature that allows volunteers within the community to be matched to events that are within their scope, including both interests and availability.
